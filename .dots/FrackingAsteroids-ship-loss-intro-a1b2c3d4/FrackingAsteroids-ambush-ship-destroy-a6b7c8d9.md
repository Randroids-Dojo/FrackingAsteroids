After the player has experienced the power showcase, trigger an ambush event.
A massive enemy force or a catastrophic event overwhelms the ship. Big dramatic
explosion, screen shake, flash to white/black. The ship is destroyed and all
upgrades are lost. This is the emotional hook — you had it all, now it's gone.
