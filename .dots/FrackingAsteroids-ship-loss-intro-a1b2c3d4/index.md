The game opens with the player piloting a fully upgraded, late-game ship — Lazer,
max storage, max collector, the works. A scripted intro sequence showcases the
ship's power: blasting through mineral-rich asteroid fields, hoovering up tons of
cargo, destroying fleets of smaller enemy ships, and zipping across the galaxy at
top speed. Then an ambush destroys the ship. The player respawns with a basic
starter ship and the existing tutorial begins.

This replaces the current cold-start tutorial flow and gives players a taste of
what they're working toward before stripping it all away.
